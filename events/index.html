Each month we run the following events:

  • 1 x Dataquest: an opportunity for teams to work on a specific problem surfaced from an accountability workshop
  • 1 x Movie Evening: a casual, informative evening where members gather to watch an informative film aligned to interesting social issues
  • 2 x Community Evenings: a chance for the Codebridge community to get together and work on projects
  • 1 x Data viz meetup: a place for people to showcase and discuss their data visualisations
  • 1 x Accountability Workshop: a workshop to understand a specific problem space, as guided by the monthly theme
