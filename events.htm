Popcorn and movie night

As part of the Right2Know Freedom Week campaign, we hosted a popcorn and movie night on the 28th of April. We watched the film Miners Shot Down, which documents the Marikana Massacre in which 44 people where killed during strike action for a living wage.

Local Budget Dataquest

We hosted a dataquest to help the National Treasury open its municipal financial data and make it widely available in order to strengthen civic oversight and public accountability as well as to promote entrepreneurship.

Open Data Day

Saturday 5 March 2016, marked International Open Data Day, a global grassroots initiative that connects people from around the world to talk about, hack and promote open data. In South Africa, Code for South Africa kicked off the day with Easter Egg Hunts in Khayelitsha at Whizz ICT Centre and Durban, headed up by our friends at Open Data Durban!
