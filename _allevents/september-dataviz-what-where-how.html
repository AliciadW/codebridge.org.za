Dataviz - the what, where and how

14 September 2016 Cape Town

Codebridge renovations are slowly becoming a fading memory and we look forward to welcoming you to September's dataviz meetup.

This month Mark Wilson will be taking us through:

  • History of data visualisation (Galileo, Jon snow, ancient cartographers)
  • Psychological mechanisms an information designer uses (Pragnatz - Gestalt laws of grouping/Gestalt Principles of design)
  • The expression of information (metadata, data information)
  • Dataviz today (digital/physical)

Hope to see you there!
