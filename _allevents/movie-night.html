Popcorn and movie night

Event Closed

We hosted our first movie night on 28 April 2016.

It formed part of the Right2Know Freedom Week campaign - in order to highlight freedom of assembly and association, the free flow of information, and the freedom of expression.

Freedom Week 2016 - 27 April (Freedom Day) to 3 May (World Press Freedom Day). Together we can defend the political space we all need to advance our struggles for social, economic, and environmental justice in the context of growing social instability and repression.

Be sure to check out the Right2Know Freedom Week website for more events and other ways of getting involved.
