Hack {code}bridge (Saturday, 27th July 2013 from 11am)

We're pushing to get the development of {code}bridge moving forward, with the aim of eventually being the ideal space for startups and like-minded hackers (software and hardware) in Cape Town.

There is a lot to get done, on the agenda for Saturday is:
* Getting consitution written for NPO ( it seems like the best way forward ) * Setting up electronic access control for the front door ( we have an arduino with an rfid reader )
* Writing up content and designing a better website
* Building the two unfinished tables that are still sitting here
* Re-wiring and chatting more about codebridges future

There will be beer, pizza, and a whole lot of hackers.

Date: July 27, 2013
Time: 11am on.
Location: codebridge - http://www.codebridge.co.za/directions.htm
