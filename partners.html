OpenUp

OpenUp uses civic technology to promote informed decision-making and drive social change. Located at {code}bridge, OpenUp focuses on opening up data and building tools to make the data available and understandable to society.

Official website

Open Data Durban

Open Data Durban is a civic technology lab that implements and advocates for open data, open government, and civic technology through projects, events, workshops, and dataquests. We work with civic society, the media, government and interested and engaged citizens.

Official website

Geekulcha

Geekulcha is where the young, skilled, creative and ambitious tech minds meet to connect with each other, share knowledge, collaborate on projects, network with industry leaders, obtain training to further improve and enhance their skills and to put that newly acquired skill to work.

Our focus is on empowering young geeks through ICT skills development and training while giving them a taste of what awaits them in the big world through industry exposure.
