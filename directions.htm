Directions

We are literally inside the Campground road bridge that intersects with Main Road in Claremont.

You can get to the parking lot by following Hemlock Street which is just off Claremont Boulvard. The parking lot is a large gravel parking lot directly adjacent to the bridge. Just follow the arrows on the map below!

The entrance is at the back corner furthest from Main Road once you're inside the parking lot.
